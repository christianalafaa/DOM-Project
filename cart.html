The Loafer Black


A modern sportswear inspired reinvention of the
classic dressy slip on. Supple Italian Nappa
leather replaces the traditional stiff box leather.
The cream translucent colored sole adds a sense
of lightness while the Native American influence
is visible in the moccasin hand stitch on the toe.
